The People’s Daily
Jan. 07, 1953

A CITY UNDER SEIGE

The industry city of Nakhovny is under fire as a rogue platoon of the Chebodny 
National Guard have barricaded themselves within the city after being deployed 
to deal with the disaster that occurred on New Years Eve. The city's economy was 
already in decline due to the ongoing war, which forced the city's workers onto 
the encroaching frontline. National Guard Lieutenant Yandarbin reports that the 
situation is under control, but provided no further comments when pressed on 
describing the immediate actions needed to de-esculate the situation. The 
Lieutenant also provided no details on what the cause of the collapse was, 
stating there are "bigger things to worry about".
