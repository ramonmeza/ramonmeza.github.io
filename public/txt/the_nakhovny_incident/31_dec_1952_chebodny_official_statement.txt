United Republic of Chebodny Security
Apanyev, Chebodny
Dec. 31, 1952

Reports of tragedy have landed upon our ears. We bleed with you, citizens of 
Nakhovny. But now is not the time for mourning, it is the time for rebuilding. 
We've deployed our finest platoon of men from the National Guard to aid in the 
disposal efforts your city is currently undergoing. While the National Guard is 
deployed in Nakhovny, the following statues from the Civil Code should at the 
forefront of each citizen's mind:

- DO NOT LEAVE YOUR DESIGNATED APARTMENT
- DO NOT INITIATE INTERATIONS WITH ANY GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL
- CARRY YOUR CITIZENSHIP PAPERS AT ALL TIMES
- ANY RATIONS WILL BE SEIZED UNTIL THE SITUATION IS RESOLVED

Violating these, or any statute of the Chebodny Civil Code will result in your 
prosecution to the fullest extent of the law.

Please remember, we are here to help. Together, we can rebuild a Nakhovny that 
will blossom into the industrious metropolis it was always meant to be.
