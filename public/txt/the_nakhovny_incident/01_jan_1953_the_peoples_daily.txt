The People’s Daily
Jan. 01, 1953

TRAGIC COLLAPSE IN NAKHOVNY

Dozens dead and Hundreds injured after the collapse of Loryol Complex in 
Nakhovny early last evening. The complex provided shelter for thousands of 
citizens. The relief effort is ongoing, but due to the scale of the disaster, it 
is unknown how long efforts will last. The United Republic of Chebodny issued a 
statement last night to reassure citizens of Nakhovny and promises of immediate 
aid from the National Guard. The statement urged citizens to stay in their 
apartments and do not try to interact with the National Guard troops, so they 
can provide the help needed.
